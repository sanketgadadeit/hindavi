CHRONICLES OF THE HINDAVI GROUP

Where we never compromise on quality.

The organization has been working under the guidance of our Managing Director MR. Dinesh R. Mangle since 2015. Mr. Dinesh Mangle is also a sole founder member of the organization and decided to start his career in Real Estate Sector and that is when the chronicles of the astonishing journey for THE HINDAVI GROUP started!
